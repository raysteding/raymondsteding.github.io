Welcome

Raymond Steding, M.A.

Raymond Steding is a researcher and educator specializing in English Rhetoric and Composition, with a focus on digital humanities and emerging technologies. He earned his Master of Arts in English from California State University, Northridge in 2019 and his Bachelor of Arts in English from UCLA in 2016.

His academic work includes research assistant positions with the Digital Humanities programs at UCSB, the University of Miami, and CSUN, as well as UCLA. His professional background spans education, network systems integration for Fortune 500 companies, and four years at Jet Propulsion Laboratories as a prototype machinist.
